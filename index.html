About me

I recently came to the realization that my current employment is not fostering the development of my innate tendencies towards curiosity, passion, exploration, and creativity. This introspection led me to question my purpose and the source of my lack of fulfillment, despite my diligent efforts and devoted energy.

After careful contemplation, I rediscovered my long-standing fascination with the intricacies of computers and their functioning. I immersed myself in extensive reading and spent countless hours in contemplative study at coffee shops. My experimentation with basic coding elicited a newfound fervor and confirmed that this was my true vocation.

With newfound inspiration, I took a calculated risk and pursued advanced studies in coding. Since then, I have been consistently honing my skills and expanding my knowledge of relevant technologies. The development of my abilities to craft clean, responsive, and legible code brings me immense satisfaction, and I eagerly anticipate continuing my journey in this ever-evolving field.
